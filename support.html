Frequently Asked Questions

How are daily earnings calculated?
Daily earnings are calculated at a fixed rate of $70 per business day (Monday to Friday). Earnings are automatically credited to your account each business day and compound over time.
When can I withdraw my earnings?
You can withdraw your earnings after the initial 6-month investment period. Withdrawal requests are processed within 3-5 business days and transferred to your registered bank account.
Is my investment secure?
Yes, all investments are protected through our secure platform. We use bank-level encryption and follow strict financial regulations to ensure the safety of your funds.
How do I update my personal information?
You can update your personal information, bank details, and contact information in the Settings section of your account. Some changes may require verification for security purposes.
What happens after 6 months?
After the initial 6-month period, you can choose to reinvest your funds, withdraw your earnings, or continue with the same investment plan. We'll contact you before the period ends with options.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, we believe in transparent pricing. There are no hidden fees. Our platform fee is included in the earnings calculation, and there are no charges for deposits or standard withdrawals.
